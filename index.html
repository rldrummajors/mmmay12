Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

Year: 2025

Show Name: To the Fullest

Group: Pride of Cincinnati

Location: WGI World Finals

Experience Level: WGI Independent World

Context: Pride of Cincinnati's "To the Fullest" is an exhilarating show featuring the music of Sleeping At Last. The circular tarp symbolizes limitations and confinement, within which the performance was restricted. By the show's conclusion, the performers broke free to utilize the entire performance space "to the fullest," embodying themes of liberation and embracing life fully. This visually compelling performance ultimately earned them a score of 94.900 and a gold medal finish.

Link: Pride of Cincinnati 2025 - To the Fullest
